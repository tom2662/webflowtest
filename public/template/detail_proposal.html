Tyler Harder

Tyler Harder is the founder and managing director of Maikai Digital. From the West coast of Canada, and residing for over 12 years in Tokyo, Tyler has directed the management and marketing of two C corporations, developed retail and digital strategy for online product launches, hospitality and tourism, and founded companies specializing in digital media marketing, app technologies, and luxury content production.

Tyler has worked extensively with both well known brands and hundreds of startup and medium sized business founders, and most enjoys bringing clarity in the expression of great ideas to small business and startup founders.

Kallah Maguire

Kallah Maguire has worked in digital media for 10 years with both small personal brands and large multinational corporations. She has worked with Disney, Microsoft, MSN Lifestyle, Fashion’s Night Out, Tommy Hilfiger, Regus, and many more.

Kallah is also considered an Instagram Influencer with a following of 42K where she is able to regularly engage with her community, gain clients, network and even make new friends. She has contributed to articles for Tyra Banks, WhoWhatWear.com, and Aston Martin, and teaches social media workshops for small business owners as well as multinational corporations.

Kallah’s passion is building strong communities around brands, and creating the Know, Like & Trust factor, so that people aren't just followers, but become an engaged and connected community.

Lana Shevchenko

Laura Abeyta has a unique understanding of what it takes to build a business from scratch. As a serial entrepreneur, having owned and operated 3 of her own companies over the past 15 years, she has a compelling sense of launch marketing. Laura now works as a consultant for product launches, marketing strategy, collaborations and startups. Her main gift is taking companies from vision to reality with practical, actionable steps forward. In her retail capacity, Laura startup and ran a series of boutique fashion outlets and acted as a tastemaker to stars such as Julia Roberts, the cast of Entourage, Twilight, regular awards shows, and was featured regularly on E! News and In-Style magazine. Laura also co-founded Hearisee Films, producing Shooting Castro, a documentary film that ran on Discovery / Times channel for 5 years. Laura’s recent activities include organizing TedX in Canggu, Bali in 2018.

Justin is a creative thinker with a passion for colour, typography, and a hands-on approach. Justin’s extensive background in graphic design, brand + identity - from conceptual thinking to illustration - gives him a unique ability to create expressive imagery that balances functionality and style.

Sindy Kirks
